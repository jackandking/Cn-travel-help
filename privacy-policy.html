Introduction

At China Travel Help, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website at cn-travel-help.cn and jackandking.github.io/Cn-travel-help.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:
