Guangzhou Travel Checklist

🏠 Home 🧒 Guangzhou for Kids 🇨🇳 China Guide
<
1. Canton Tower (广州塔)

The iconic twisted tower of Guangzhou, also known as "Little Waist" for its unique shape. Experience breathtaking views of the Pearl River.

2. Chen Clan Ancestral Hall (陈家祠)

A masterpiece of Lingnan architecture showcasing traditional Cantonese craftsmanship and folk art.

3. Shamian Island (沙面岛)

A charming colonial-era island with European-style buildings and tree-lined streets perfect for strolling.

4. Pearl River Cruise (珠江夜游)

Experience Guangzhou's skyline from the water, especially magical at night when the city lights reflect on the river.

5. Yuexiu Park (越秀公园)

The largest park in central Guangzhou, home to the famous Five Rams Sculpture and Zhenhai Tower.

6. Sacred Heart Cathedral (石室圣心大教堂)

A stunning Gothic cathedral built entirely of granite, known as the "Stone House" by locals.

7. Food Adventure - Dim Sum & Cantonese Cuisine

Guangzhou is the birthplace of dim sum and home to authentic Cantonese cuisine!

8. Beijing Road Pedestrian Street (北京路步行街)

A bustling shopping street with over 2000 years of history, featuring both modern stores and traditional architecture.

🎯 Bonus: Cultural Experiences

Try to complete at least 3 of these authentic Guangzhou experiences!
