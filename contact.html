Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often do you update the travel checklists?
A: We review and update our checklists regularly, typically every few months or when we receive reliable reports about changes to attractions, restaurants, or travel conditions.
Q: Can you add a checklist for [specific city]?
A: We're always looking to expand our coverage! Please send us your suggestion through our feedback form or GitHub discussions, and we'll consider it for future updates.
Q: Why isn't my checklist progress saving?
A: Checklist progress is saved locally in your browser. Make sure you're using the same device and browser, and that you haven't cleared your browser data. The feature works best with cookies and local storage enabled.
Q: Is the travel information current?
A: While we strive to keep information current, travel conditions can change rapidly. Always verify important details like visa requirements, opening hours, and health recommendations with official sources before traveling.
