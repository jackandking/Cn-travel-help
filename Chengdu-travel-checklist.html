1. Giant Panda Research Base (大熊猫繁育研究基地)

The world's largest panda breeding center. Home to over 100 giant pandas and their adorable cubs!

2. Wuhou Shrine (武侯祠)

Dedicated to Zhuge Liang, the legendary strategist from the Three Kingdoms period.

3. Jinli Ancient Street (锦里古街)

A recreated ancient street full of traditional crafts, snacks, and Sichuan culture.

4. Wide and Narrow Alleys (宽窄巷子)

Historic neighborhood with Qing Dynasty architecture, trendy shops, and local life.

5. Sichuan Cuisine Experience

Chengdu is the UNESCO City of Gastronomy - prepare for amazing spicy flavors!

6. Mount Qingcheng (青城山)

One of the birthplaces of Taoism, known for its serene temples and lush mountains.

🎯 Cultural Immersion Challenge

Complete at least 4 of these to truly experience Chengdu culture!
