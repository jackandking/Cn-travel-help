How to Visit the Forbidden City in Just 3 Hours

🏠 Home 🏛️ Beijing Checklist

A lot of first-time travelers to Beijing ask: "Is the Forbidden City too big to see in one day?"

The truth is, you don't need 6–7 hours inside. With the right route, you can cover the highlights in about 3 hours — even if you're traveling with kids.

Here's the route that worked really well for us:

🎫 1. Tickets & Entry

Essential preparation for your Forbidden City visit.

💡 Pro Tip
The official Palace Museum website and WeChat mini-program are the best ways to book tickets. Book as early as possible, especially for weekends and holidays!
🗺️ 2. 3-Hour Route

Follow this efficient path to see the Forbidden City's highlights without getting overwhelmed.

🏯 Step 1: Meridian Gate → Hall of Supreme Harmony (Taihe Dian)
👑 Step 2: Palace of Heavenly Purity (Qianqing Gong)
🌿 Step 3: Imperial Garden (Yu Hua Yuan)
🚪 Step 4: Exit through Gate of Divine Might (Shenwu Men)
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 3. Family-Friendly Tips

Make your Forbidden City visit enjoyable for the whole family.

👶 Kid-Friendly Tip
Strollers are allowed, but the stone steps can be tricky — a baby carrier is easier to navigate through the palace complex.
🍜 4. Nearby Lunch Spot

Where to eat after your Forbidden City visit.

🍽️ Dining Tip
Right outside the north gate (Shenwu Men), you'll find a few small local restaurants and cafes — perfect for a quick bite before heading to your next stop.
