🏔️ Zhangjiajie Travel Checklist

🏠 Home 🧒 Zhangjiajie for Kids 🇨🇳 China Guide
1. Zhangjiajie National Forest Park (张家界国家森林公园) - Avatar Mountains

The world-famous pillar-like mountains that inspired the floating peaks in James Cameron's Avatar movie.

2. Tianmen Mountain (天门山) - Heaven's Gate

Famous for its natural arch "Heaven's Gate" and the world's longest cable car ride.

3. Zhangjiajie Glass Bridge (张家界玻璃桥)

The world's longest and highest glass-bottomed bridge, offering spectacular canyon views.

4. Tianzi Mountain (天子山) - Emperor's Mountain

Known for its sea of clouds and spectacular sunrise views over the pillar mountains.

5. Yellow Dragon Cave (黄龙洞)

One of China's largest karst caves, featuring underground rivers and stunning formations.

6. Zhangjiajie Cuisine & Culture

Experience the unique flavors of Hunan province and local Tujia minority culture.
