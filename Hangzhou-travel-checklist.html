Hangzhou Travel Checklist

<
1. West Lake (西湖) - UNESCO World Heritage Site

One of China's most celebrated natural beauty spots, inspiring poets and artists for centuries.

2. Lingyin Temple (灵隐寺)

One of China's most famous Buddhist temples, nestled in the hills around West Lake.

3. Longjing Tea Culture Experience

Hangzhou is the birthplace of the famous Dragon Well green tea.

4. Hefang Street (河坊街)

Historic pedestrian street showcasing traditional Hangzhou culture and crafts.

5. Xixi National Wetland Park (西溪湿地)

A rare urban wetland offering peaceful nature experiences within the city.

6. Hangzhou Cuisine Adventure

Discover the delicate flavors of Zhejiang cuisine, one of China's eight major culinary traditions.

Complete at least 4 of these to truly experience Hangzhou's refined culture!
